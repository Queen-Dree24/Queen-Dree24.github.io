Our Fitness Programs

At DESTI FITNESS & HEALTH, we offer a diverse range of programs designed to cater to all fitness levels and preferences. Whether you prefer the energy of a group class, the focus of personalized training, or the convenience of online sessions, we have something to help you achieve your goals.

Achieve Your Fitness Goals in Our Classes

Aerobics Classes

Boost your cardiovascular health and endurance with our fun and dynamic aerobics sessions. High-energy movements set to music, perfect for improving stamina and burning calories.

  • Format: Group Class
  • Availability: Online & In-Person
  • Great for: Cardiovascular fitness, endurance, mood boosting.

Strength & Cardio Classes

Combine the best of both worlds! These classes blend cardiovascular bursts with strength-building exercises to maximize fat loss, improve muscle tone, and enhance overall fitness.

  • Format: Group Class
  • Availability: Online & In-Person
  • Great for: Full-body conditioning, fat burning, increasing power.

Weight Training Sessions

Build lean muscle, increase strength, and reshape your body with our expertly guided weight training programs. Suitable for all levels, learn proper form and effective techniques.

  • Format: Group Class & Personal Training (available)
  • Availability: In-Person (Online options for specific focused sessions)
  • Great for: Muscle building, strength development, bone density.

Cycling Classes

Get ready to ride! Our indoor cycling classes offer an exhilarating low-impact, high-intensity cardio workout. Cycle to the rhythm and challenge yourself for improved stamina and leg strength.

  • Format: Group Class
  • Availability: In-Person
  • Great for: Endurance, low-impact cardio, leg strength.

Calisthenics Workshops

Master your bodyweight with our calisthenics workshops. Develop incredible strength, flexibility, and control using only your own body, perfect for functional fitness.

  • Format: Group Class & Specialized Workshops
  • Availability: Online & In-Person
  • Great for: Functional strength, body control, flexibility.

Personal Training & Custom Programs

Beyond our group classes, we also offer personalized one-on-one training and custom program design. Contact us to discuss your individual needs and create a plan tailored just for you!
